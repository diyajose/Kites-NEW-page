KALAM CENTRE

The Future Project

The Future Project is the lab of Social Innovators where young talented minds can pitch their theory of change and work collaboratively for it, be it product-led, community-led or volunteer-led innovative ideas creating local solutions for local problems. The initiative will also introduce youth to the countless opportunities they can pursue in the development industry with informed choices. This is a unique opportunity to make an impact, learn, network, and improve social skills.

This comprehensive support platform enables youngsters to take charge of their own ideas, innovate in their own ways, and build a group of doers to achieve their bigger social goals. The initiative is also about harnessing the power of exponential technology with the help of expert advisors and learning programs in the non-profit sector to work in a futuristic manner



To know more about The Future Project!

Kalam Fellowship

Kalam Fellowship is an opportunity for India’s brightest and most promising young talents to serve as full-timers and utilize their skills and entrepreneurial mindset to serve underserved communities in the rural hinterland of the country. Fellows get exposed to the grassroots realities of India’s rural system and will have an opportunity to cultivate the knowledge, skills, and mindsets necessary to attain positions of leadership.

SARVODAYA

Empowering rural communities for a sustainable future

The core idea of the program is to provide innovative solutions to deep-rooted development problems in the rural hinterland of our country by building the capacity of communities through sustainable and holistic interventions in Health, Livelihood, Education, Environmental Sustainability, Technology and Disaster Resilience with decentralised planning and implementation at the grassroots levels, enabling and empowering individuals to articulate their need.

KITES COMMUNITY OUTREACH

Communities of Practice

A community of practice (CoP) is a group of people who share a common problem, a set of problems, or an interest in a topic and fulfil both individual and group goals by learning and practising together. They often focus on sharing best practices and creating new knowledge to advance a domain of professional practice. CoPs in KITES will fundamentally organise members to different interest groups and connect them with practitioners and domain experts to enable learning, knowledge sharing and networking for opportunities




To Know more about Communities of Practice!

Kites Campus

One of the core areas of focus is KITES Campus, an initiative in partnership with higher education institutions to extend Community Service Learning to all students universalising positive and creative social interventions and social volunteering. Often social initiatives are restricted and thought through as a responsibility of a few in such higher education institutions. Breaking the convention, KITES is keen to support collective participation, and community building and thereby nurture socially conscious and responsible students.
