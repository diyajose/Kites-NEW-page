Microlearning Hubs •

A comprehensive education crisis runs wide and deep in our country.


Microlearning Hubs strives to bridge this gap and serve as experiential learning centres for rural children by offering free academic support.

ENVIORNMENT

Ecosystem Restoration Project•

For the revival of Perunthodu, situated in Kaipamangalam, Kerala a disaster-resilient, evident-based, community-led model with economic integration to ensure inclusivity and sustainability was envisioned. Executed in collaboration with the College of Climate Change and Environmental Science (CCCES), KAU and the local self-government, the project was followed by a five-year plan to promote sustainable agricultural practices and integration of disaster-resilient structures

Punarjjani •

Campaign to promote food self-sufficiency in the state. Punarjjani, with the help of experts, strives to educate households on the inevitability of attaining self-sufficiency in food production.

TECHNOLOGY

Munnar VIBGYOR Tourism•

Theme-based, circuit tourism project developed by KITES and executed in collaboration with District Administration, Idukki for enhancing the tourism experience. A social hackathon ‘Code4Munnar’ was conducted to develop ‘Hello Munnar’ an open-source web app for improved discoverability. KITES is also collaborating with UNDP and Haritha Keralam Mission to integrate eco-friendly choices into the tourism model.

ART AND CULTURE

Rang •

Rang - enabled by technology, Kerala’s largest virtual youth festival features over 160 events in three different categories. In the 2021 edition, there were more than 1500 participants from various districts of Kerala.

Kites Books •

A zero-cost book publishing project that opens the door to aspiring writers to publish their books without spending a lump sum amount. This revolutionary concept is designed to support those who are unable to approach or convince a publisher for various reasons.

Voice Of India •

A virtual platform for youngsters from across India to express their innovative ideas to build a better nation. Participants can speak in their regional language and will be streamed on the official Social Media handles of KITES.
