About us

Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Build together a livable planet, inclusive, equitable, and sustainable through youth-led development and community-driven action

Our Aspiration

Build a unique functional model of the organization that can create scalable social impact at the national and international levels.

Our Mission

  • Create social entrepreneurs capable of responding to new challenges.
  • Join hands with state and non-state actors, academia, industry, and civil society.
  • Engage in quality knowledge generation to facilitate informed action.
  • Make the most of youth networking opportunities.
  • Form Communities of Practice to enable learning.
  • Build self-reliant and sustainable model villages through youth intervention.

About Kites Foundation
