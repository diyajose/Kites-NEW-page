Kites Foundation

As a youth ecosystem enabling service-led, volunteer-led and community-led social innovations, KITES is where inspired young minds come together to share innovative ideas that have unparalleled relevance in the age of the Sustainable Development Goals. The model envisioned by KITES integrates the following:

  • Community building and youth networking
  • Communities of Practice
  • Social Innovation and Entrepreneurship
  • Rural Development through SDG Localization
  • Partnerships and stakeholder engagement for common goals

Initiatives in all these areas span various socio-developmental sectors like Education and Skilling Industry, Climate Action and Environmental Sustainability, Health and Sanitation, Art and Culture, Livelihood Generation and Socially Responsible Tech, etc. They are also guided by the 17 Sustainable Development Goals which are comprehensive, far-reaching, and people-centred set of universal goals.
